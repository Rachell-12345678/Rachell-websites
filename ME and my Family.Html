MY STORY

HEY My name is Rachel Sahle, and I was born in Mekelle.
I am the firstborn child of Sahle Araya and Genet Tsegay.
I grew up in a large family that included my grandmother, uncles, aunts, and my parents.
However, I was especially close with my uncles and aunts.
My relationship with them was stronger than with my parents.
As a child, I spent every weekend with my uncle.
we enjoyed various activities together, such as visiting different parks and game zones, as well as swimming.
Some of the places we frequently visited included Dejen Park, Abrhakastl , Dove Cafe, Hilltop, Da Raesi Park, and Da Tafere Park (Eilalal)etc...
I have unforgettable memories from those outings.
I also loved caring for, feeding, and washing animals when I was young.
The animals I took care of included dogs, cats, birds, and even a monkey.
Additionally, I had a great relationship with my neighborshoods.
My uncles and aunts have always been my role models and have played a significant part in shaping who I am today.
My parents have also played a crucial role in my life.
by fulfilling all my needs and wants, helping me become the person I am now.
