My Elementary-preparatory

I started a new chapter at Abba Gebre Michael Catholic School ,From grades 1 to 12.
I was a curious and clever student in the lower grades. However, after grade 6, I became a dedicated and serious student.
I took the general exam in grade 8 and passed with excellent grades, which allowed me to begin grade 9 with new courses and classes.
Subsequently, I spent two years learning English at the A Plus Language Center.
Afterward, I took the grade 10 national matriculation exam and achieved great results as well.
In grade 11, I focused on a natural science course, which included new subjects along with a technical drawing class, and I worked hard to excel.
In grade 12, I returned to the language center where I had studied in grade 10 to complete my education and receive my diploma.
After that, I took the Grade 12 matriculation exam and was accepted into Meles Campus at Mekelle University.
